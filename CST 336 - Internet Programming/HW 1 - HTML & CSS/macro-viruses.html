MACRO VIRUSES:

What are Macro Viruses?
  • Macro viruses occur in sowtware applications, like in Microsoft Office.
  • They attack by forcefully inserting their code into macros through data files, but the attack is initiated by a human interaction, unknowingly of course.
  • Ultimately, once a macro virus is successfully entered into a user's device, they have the power to:
  • Damage files by deleting, displacing, and moving them.
  • Sending files with private and personal information/data.
  • Formatting storage drives and intentionally messing with them.
Ways to Prevent a Macro Virus Attack:
  • In order to prevent and remove macro viruses off of your device, you can:
  • Install a virus/malware scanner on your device so it can detect anything suspicious.
  • If a virus is found, clean it from your computer as soon as possible to avoid the leaking of all of your information.
  • By checking and scanning your device frequently, you have a higher chance of securing your personal and private files from macro virus attacks.
