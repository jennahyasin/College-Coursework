BROWSER HIJACKER VIRUS:

What are Browser Hijacker Viruses?
  • Browser Hijacker Viruses take place in browsers where overall, mallicious companies alter their browsers to intentionally program them to redirect and open other unwanted tabs that can invade a user's personal privacy.
  • A browser hijacker's ultimate goal is to forcefully hack into user's devices through messing with a browser's original set up.
  • A list of objectives of a browser hijacker are:
  • To get into a user's device and grasp their information
  • To spy on a user's activity
  • To advertise unwanted advertisements by the user constantly
Ways to Prevent a Browser Hijack Attack:
  • In order to avoid having your browser hijacked, there are some things that can be done to prevent the occurance of this mallicious act:
  • Installing antivirus software that will notify you when there is something suspicious going on.
  • Frequently cleaning browser cookies and history.
  • Avoid installing software that doesn't seem reliable since it can potentially involve some hijacking attempts.
  • Updating your browser whenever notfied to do so.
